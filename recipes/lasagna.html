Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish featuring alternating layers of flat pasta sheets, meat-based ragù, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and shredded mozzarella cheese. It is a quintessential comfort food, usually baked until bubbling and golden, and is often prepared in large batches for gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef

Steps

  • Preparing a meat or marinara sauce
  • Mixing ricotta with egg/cheese
  • Layering them with noodles, mozzarella, and parmesan in a 9x13 pan
  • Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–40 minutes, covered, then 5–10 minutes uncovered until bubbly.
