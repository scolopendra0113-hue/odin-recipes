Tomato Soup

Description

Tomato soup is a classic, comforting dish featuring a rich, velvety blend of savory tomatoes, often enhanced with onions, garlic, and herbs like basil.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Broth

Steps

  • Make creamy, homemade tomato soup by roasting 2-3 lbs of tomatoes, 1 onion, and 4-6 garlic cloves at 400°F with olive oil for 30-40 minutes.
  • Blend the roasted veggies with 2-4 cups of veggie/chicken broth until smooth.
  • Simmer in a pot, adding optional heavy cream or fresh basil for richness, then serve hot.
